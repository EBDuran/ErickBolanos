About me

Hello, and welcome!strong> My name is Erick Bolaños Duran, Evolutionary biologist specializing in marine ecology. My research leverages molecular tools, multivariate statistics, and machine learning to characterize the seascape. I aim to uncover cryptic diversity and understand the genomic basis of adaptation in wild populations. Ultimately, my goal is to apply conservation genomics to assess population resilience and predict responses to future climate change scenarios. Please reach out if you have any questions about my work, I am always happy to connect!
